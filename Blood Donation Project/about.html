About Kenketsu
- It’s started the journey in October 2020 with the unique concept of helping people by
 donating blood. Blood donation for saving live has created a milestone among general
 people. Kenketsu provides the safest possible blood for the patients which follows the
 American Association of Blood Bank (AABB) guideline, USFDA guideline is used for donor
 infectious diseases screening (HBs Ag, HIV-1 &II, Anti – HCV, VDRL, Malaria).
 Kenketsu is totally online based organization.

The motive of Kenketsu

  *To make a database of blood donors.
  *To collect blood from public blood donors.
  *To support voluntary blood donation campaigns.
  *To provide emergency blood support to the general public.
  *To help the poor by providing emergency blood service.
